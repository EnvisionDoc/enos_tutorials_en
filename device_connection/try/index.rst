Tutorial: Connecting devices into EnOS through EnOS Edge
================================================================

This tutorial walks you through the connection of devices into EnOS cloud through cloud-based EnOS edge.

Audience
-------------------

IoT engineers

Duration
-------------------

30 min

Objectives
-------------------

- Objective 1: Simulating an IoT device with your laptop
- Objective 2: Configuring the connection to EnOS through EnOS cloud edge.
- Objective 3: Debugging connection and previewing data.
